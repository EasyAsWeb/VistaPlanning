Our Services

We offer you a professional and responsive resource consenting service for proposals involving subdivisions, cross lease updates and amendments, new dwellings, extensions and alterations, commercial, and existing use activities.

 

We can:

  • Complete a resource consent feasibility assessment relating to your project.
  • Work with specialists such as your architect, engineer, or surveyor to ensure the easiest consenting journey possible for your development.
  • Attend pre-application meetings with Council staff to ensure early feedback is incorporated in to your proposal.
  • Prepare a quality application including a thorough assessment of environmental effects, lodge this with Council, and keep you updated all the way.
  • Attend promptly to any Council s92 requests for further information.
  • Review and provide feedback to yourself and Council planners on draft conditions of resource consent.

 

We want to make your resource consent journey as simple as possible, and we will work with you and Council to get your project consented and underway.
