About Us

Vista Planning is led by Andreas Lilley, a qualified planner holding NZPI membership, who has over sixteen years of Auckland local government experience involving project delivery, resource consent processing, peer review and signoff. Andreas and the team know what is required to get your project through the planning approval process.

Andreas has a thorough working knowledge of the Resource Management Act, related legislation, and the Auckland Unitary Plan, and keeps up to date with all the changes to consent requirements in this ever changing profession.
